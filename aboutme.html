About Me

How I Came To Be a "Girl Mom"

My name is Amber Davis and I have been married to my husband for 14 years. I grew up knowing that I wanted to be a mom,
but I don't think that I ever dreamed that I would have four children....and all of them be girls.
Soon after getting married, I got my first teaching job. During my first year teaching, I found out I was, happily, expecting!
My first daughter, Cali, came in May 2013. Quickly after Cali's arrival, my next daughter, Caroline, made her appearnace in October 2014.
I was knee deep into being a mom quickly with two toddlers under the age of 2. I was still working and had become a kindergarten teacher.
Our plan was to see what happened as far as having a third child, but we did not have to wait long. Next arrived our Carrigan shortly after Caroline
in May 2017. While I don't think we ever truly believed that we would actually have a fourth, we never pushed the idea totally out of our minds.
Unexpectedly, but with pure joy, we welcomed baby daughter number four, Camree, in October 2019. Yes, you read that right...
FOUR daughters!
